The Shadow Knights Incoporated, also known as TSK Inc. serves as a creative vessel for worldbuilding and storytelling. This project is helmed by Collin Floyd, along with artist Robert Yap, as well as coproducers Austin Carver, Alvin Hong, and Kevin Heyn. This project has been in development for over 3 years now, originating in August of 2019.

Core Mechanics within TSK define the major concepts that the game is based off of. Magical elements, known as Affinities, is the largest of the mechanics. Roles, Miscellaneous Skills, Reforges, and Player Stats are also of the largest mechanics within the story.

Though The Shadow Knights has been simply a passion project for the past few years, it may evolve into more within the future. Ideas speculated include converting the story to a campaign of Dungeons & Dragons, as well as possibly making it into a full RPG video game.
