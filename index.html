Problem Statement: College Schedules

How can college studebts better develop tools or systems to help them manage their schedules and assignments more effectively?

Affinity Diagram: Inconvenient Schedules

The affinity diagram contains six sections. These sections refer to the structure of our system, the marketing, the accessibility of this system, the hurdles that we may need to face with this system, and some features that will definitely be included.


Persona: Kevin

Kevin is a college undergraduate looking for a more efficient way to get food and snacks. He spends most of his time on schoolwork and games, so he needs a time-saving solution to his culinary needs. "I need snacks, ASAP!"

Storyboard: Demeter Grocer

Kevin's issue of no snacks is solved once he downloads Demeter Grocer, allowing him to quickly and efficiently get food.


Sketch: Demeter Grocer App

Demeter Grocer hosts a direct interface, allowing users to search for specific items and stores, then request a ride to and from them.
