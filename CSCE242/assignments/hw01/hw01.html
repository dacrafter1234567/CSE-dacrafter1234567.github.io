The World of Arconia

Welcome to the world of Arconia! A magic-filled planet split into eight different continents each with their own diverse regions and kingdoms.
The land is rich with ancient secrets, forgotten relics, and the remnants of a cataclysmic war between divine beings that shaped its very foundation.
Empires rise and fall under the watchful eyes of the deities, their wills shaping the lives of mortals, for better or worse.

Within the world of Arconia, there exists 23 magical elements, known as "Affinities." Each of these affinities represent the godly domains dedicated to
each of the deities that exists in the realm. Everything from the natural elements such as fire, water, wind, and earth, to the more metaphysical domains
such as gravity, illusion, and shadow, show how diverse the spectrum of the world's magic is. Though the gods of the realm no longer physically exist in the
world due to a war of old, their presence is still seen today, channeled through the powers of capable heroes and villains of Arconia.
